Quantified Data Infographic

Background & Solution

Asked to display averaged data sets tracking classmates’ time and spending habits, the designer created an infographic based on proportionate ratios.


Though the data presented is highly personal, the infographic’s aesthetic relays an individual’s retained anonymity. Once averaged into a vast quantified data sample, a person’s personal data blends into the large data set.
