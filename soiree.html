Soiree Logotype

Background & Solution

Coastal Cocktails Inc. enlisted a design group to name and brand their new gourmet cocktail mixer line.


Soiree's finished word mark emphasizes sophistication and poise, with a hint of mischief. The swash is modeled after a martini peel, and references patrons peeling away their worries as they unwind at a party.
